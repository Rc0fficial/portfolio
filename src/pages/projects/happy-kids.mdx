**Description:**

Happy Kids Dental is a responsive web app designed to provide comprehensive information on pediatric dental services, including decay prevention, fluoride treatments, dental fillings, crowns, sealants, tooth extractions, and emergency care. The app also offers patient resources and appointment scheduling, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience.

I contributed to the development of this project by collaborating with the team to create the front-end using React and Next.js. My responsibilities included implementing responsive designs with Tailwind CSS, managing application state with Redux Toolkit, integrating video content with Video.js, and incorporating dynamic content with Strapi.

- **Video.js**
